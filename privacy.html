Privacy Policy — UnaKotse

Last updated: July 2026

UnaKotse ("the app") helps first-time car buyers in the Philippines compare car brands, prices, and specifications.

Information we collect

UnaKotse does not require an account and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The app does not access your contacts, location, camera, microphone, or files.

Data the app loads

The app retrieves publicly available car catalog data (prices and specifications) from a published spreadsheet in order to display up-to-date listings. This request does not include any personal information about you.

Third-party links

The app contains links to official car brand websites. Once you leave the app, those websites' own privacy policies apply.

Children

The app is a general-audience reference tool and does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If the app's data practices change (for example, if optional features like saved favorites are added), this policy will be updated before those features go live.

Contact

For questions about this policy, contact the developer through the app's Google Play listing page.
